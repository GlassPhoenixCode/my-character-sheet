Player Characters

Changing this setting allows you to quickly visualize how swingy your encounter is, by simulating what happens if your players are slightly luckier or slightly more unlucky than average.

A luck factor of +1 means instead of rolling 10 on average, the players will roll 11 on average, and their enemies will roll 9 on average.
